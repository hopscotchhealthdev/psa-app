Frequently Asked Questions's

How do I share videos?

In order to share videos, follow the steps in this linked video. 

How do I stop recording a video challenge?

The video challenges and scripts are designed to fit a certain length of time. Thus, a recording cannot be stopped mid-way. You can re-record once the current video has run its course. 

Why does my camera stay on even after I have completed the video challenge?

We are constantly upgrading our website. Currently, the camera stays on until you close the tab, but don’t worry, it only records when you take a challenge. 

Why am I unable to record a challenge?
If you are using a Safari browser:

You need to change some system preferences. Watch the linked videos to learn how to change your settings on an iPhone and a Mac. The changes in settings are only successful if you refresh your web page after. 
‍
If this is unsuccessful, you will likely need to update Safari to version 13.1 or higher. Please note that on a few select devices, you would still be unable to record your challenge. We are working on it - check back in a few days!

If you are using a Chrome browser

The video challenges are only compatible with Chrome version 80 or higher. Update Chrome, and you’re all set! Please note that on a few select devices, you would still be unable to record your challenge. We are working on it - check back in a few days!
Users who previously accepted a video challenge have been unsuccessful in using Chrome on an Apple device. We recommend that you use Safari on an Apple device and Chrome on an Android.
