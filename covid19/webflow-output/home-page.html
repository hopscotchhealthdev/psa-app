Why Record KaroNa?

We make it easy and fun for you to broadcast health awareness messaging to your community. 
As a result of social media, community leaders have considerable influence. A Stanford paper found that an effective campaign would involve community leaders capitalising on information from credible sources, and sharing it in their own voices. 

Learning from research like this, and looking back on successful public health campaigns like, Amitabh Bachchan’s Pulse Polio initiative and Development Media International's radio campaigns - we put together Record KaroNa.

Disseminate important messages

Gone are the days where you need to collect your thoughts before recording a message for the public. Creating a video is as easy as turning on your camera and reading a pre-written script.

Researched and verified

In the few minutes you spend on creating a video challenge, you will be joining our commitment to advance scientifically-accurate and publicly-relevant information sourced from sourced COVID-19 Health Literacy Project within our communities.

Multi-language recording

The best part? Each video challenge is supported in multiple languages making sure the information is accessible to everyone.
